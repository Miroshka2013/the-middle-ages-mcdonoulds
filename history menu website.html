📜 About the Middle Ages 📜

The Middle Ages lasted from about 500 AD to 1500 AD. During this time, Europe was ruled by kings and queens. Knights defended castles, and most people worked as farmers. Great cathedrals were built, and the Black Death changed history forever.

Food was simple for peasants but rich and heavily spiced for nobles. There were no fast-food restaurants — until now!
